About Me

I've always been drawn to creative projects. After working in retail, hospitality and finance sectors, I  stepped away to grow my skillset. Having started in a creative field I wanted to reincorporate my former studies with my current business knowledge and experience and further challenge myself. I have always believed that my positive outlook and love for learning allows me to do my job well. No matter the industry - finding solutions to my clients problems has always been my key driver

And when I'm not working my passions have always extended to the arts: painting, drawing and photography to be specific. I have spent many happy hours editing families I captured through the lens.
        My little family is both my driving force and my biggest fans, whether I'm coding or behind the lens.
        I wanted a career that allows me to work hard, but still be able to incorporate my love of art and design. I also wanted one that would allow me to spend quality time with my family.
        I am excited to work in a company that embodies these goals.
